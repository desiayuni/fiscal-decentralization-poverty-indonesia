\# Replication Summary



\## Paper

Impact of Village Savings and Loans Associations: Evidence from a Cluster Randomized Trial



\## Journal

Journal of Development Economics (2016)



\## Objective

This replication project reproduces selected treatment effect estimations from the original VSLA randomized controlled trial study using STATA.



\## Main Specification

\- OLS regression

\- Clustered standard errors at village level

\- Block fixed effects included

\- Post-treatment sample



\## Main Result

Estimated treatment effect on loan access:



0.122



P-value:



0.002



\## Interpretation

The replication suggests that households assigned to the VSLA treatment group experienced a statistically significant increase in loan access relative to the control group.
